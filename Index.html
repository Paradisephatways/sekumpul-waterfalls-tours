Where Is Fiji and Sekumpul Waterfall

Sekumpul and Fiji Waterfall is also located in the Buleleng Regency of Bali, Indonesia. It is situated in the village of Lemukih, which is approximately 66 kilometers north of Denpasar, the capital city of Bali. The waterfall is renowned for its majestic beauty, consisting of a cluster of several cascades that flow through the lush jungle. To reach Sekumpul and Fiji Waterfall, visitors usually embark on a trek through picturesque landscapes, crossing rivers and rice terraces along the way. It is a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and photographers looking to capture the enchanting beauty of Bali's waterfalls.

HOW TO GET TO FIJI AND SEKUMPUL WATERFALL

Although Fiji and Sekumpul waterfall is one of the best attractions in Bali, it’s a little off the beaten track and quite hard to get to. Therefore, we recommend hiring a driver to Lemukih Village, Be warned though; the road to Sekumpul waterfall is very windy and steep in parts and is the main route between the north and south, making it extremely busy throughout the day.
